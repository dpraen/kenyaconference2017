JOHANNESBURG
Day 2

SANDTON CONVENTION CENTRE

We recognize that every delegate attending the SYSPRO Customer Event comes with a different objective in mind.

The Johannesburg event takes place over two days – The 17th and 18th of May. The 17th of May will consist of plenary sessions and the 18th of May will allow for breakaway sessions. You will have the opportunity to create and manage your event agenda on the 18th of May.  

Please join us on the 17th of May for the cocktail party directly after the event at the Sandton Convention Centre. This is a great opportunity to meet the SYSPRO team and industry colleagues.
