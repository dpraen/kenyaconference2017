Speakers & Bios

Hosted by Gareth Cliff , we’ll hear from Professor(name), leader in (please advise), Microsoft CMO, (please advise) as well as share the inside track of SYSPROs Innovation team with some of the latest in cloud and AI technology.

Speakers

Learn about current trends and future technologies and how to Connect the Dots in your business.
Our speakers will focus on how SYSPRO is transforming and empowering our customers to achieve more.
PHIL
DUFF
Chief Executive Officer – SYSPRO Corporate
About Speaker:

At a time when many technology entrepreneurs felt that success was dependent on a quick IPO, Phil, and his brother Chris, identified a gap in the market and founded SYSPRO. Today, SYSPRO is one of the longest standing independent vendors of ERP software. Phil has always believed in the long term, rather than quick profits and this philosophy has established SYSPRO as a specialist in the manufacturing and distribution industries.

MARK 
WILSON
MANAGING DIRECTOR
SYSPRO AFRICA
About Speaker:

Mark has over 20 years of professional experience within the IT industry and considers SYSPRO’s differentiator is its customer-centric approach, always taking the time to understand and recognise customers' specific needs. Having joined SYSPRO in January, Mark is drawing on his extensive experience in the Africa region to define key growth areas and develop new partnerships to extend SYSPRO's footprint across the continent.

DION
CHANG
Trends Expert & Founder at Flux Trends
About Speaker:

Dion Chang is an innovator, creative thinker and walking ideas bank. He is one of South Africa’s most respected trend analysts and takes the unique view of “trends as business strategy”. In a world struggling to adapt to a new world order, his trends analysis company – Flux Trends – specializes in understanding consumer mindset and identifying unexpected business opportunities within shifting trends, ensuring that global trends have relevance when translated for South African businesses.

PAULO
DE MATOS
Chief Product
Officer, SYSPRO
About Speaker:

Paulo has played an integral role in the SYSPRO Community for 24 years, originally as a technical developer and thereafter in a business development role.  He has accumulated vast international and industry experience over the years and is responsible for ensuring that maximum business value is achieved from SYSPRO’s offerings in the market.

KEVIN
DHERMAN
Chief Innovation
Officer, SYSPRO
About Speaker:

Kevin Dherman plays a key role in defining SYSPRO’s architecture and solution development. He believes that research and development is a key component of innovation and devotes much time investigating new technologies, integration strategies and development.  Kevin works closely with the Microsoft development team and is regularly invited to lecture at Microsoft Tech-Ed and Microsoft Developer Days.

ALOK
AJMERA
President & Chief Operating Officer, Prophix
About Speaker:

Alok is responsible for ensuring that every customer, prospective customer, partner, and employee around the globe has a memorable and positive experience with Prophix and its products. He unites a team of senior executives spanning Customer Service, Global Sales, and Marketing – inspiring them to achieve greatness that exceeds all expectations.

LOUISE
THOMPSON
Chief Services
Officer, SYSPRO
About Speaker:

Louise joined SYSPRO in 1985 and has been closely involved in the company’s product development as well as providing services globally. Given the extensive nature of an ERP system, Louise is of the strong belief that the support and services provided by SYSPRO must simplify the customer experience.

MARK
GREGORY
CEO of LYNQ
About Speaker:

Father of two girls and a sailing enthusiast… Driven by results, Mark has assisted some of the world’s leading manufacturing companies to significantly improve their operations. During his 20 year career in manufacturing he has held several senior management roles and has a strong blend of systems experience.

DOUG
HUNTER
Product Services Manager, SYSPRO
About Speaker:

Doug, an Industrial Engineer from Glasgow, moved through Production, Logistics and General Management in the UK and South Africa before joining the IT Industry. He’s a manufacturing/logistics business professional at heart - rather than an IT techie. His passion is to help companies discover and implement innovation using people, processes and appropriate business IT.
