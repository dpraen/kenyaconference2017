WHY PARTICIPATE

Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend the event?

The event is for C-Suite Executives, managers from across your business including IT, operations, supply chain, manufacturing and sales. Delegates that attend want to keep abreast of the latest trends and innovations shaping their industries, make valuable contacts and exchange experiences with the SYSPRO’s ecosystem of experts, partners and customers.

Is there a charge to attend?

No, this event is free for our customers and partners

When and where will the conference be held?

Johannesburg: 17 & 18 May 2017 @ the Sandton Convention Centre - Click here to view venue
Cape Town: 23 May at Century City - Click here to view venue
Durban: 26 May 2017 @ Durban ICC - Click here to view venue

How do I register?

Register by clicking the "Register Now" button above. This will bring you to the registration page.

Where do I find information on the agenda?

Information regarding the agenda and speakers is available under the ‘Agenda’ tab.

Is there an evening cocktail party?

Yes, there is a cocktail party directly after the event, so avoid the traffic and join us for a drink, canapés, and some entertainment. This is a great opportunity meet the SYSPRO team and mingle with industry colleagues. Please refer to the Agenda for further information.

What is the dress code?

Smart casual business attire.

Can I bring a colleague?

You are very welcome to bring a colleague, please make sure he / she registers prior to the event, so that we can accommodate him or her appropriately – click here to register.

Is there a two day agenda for Cape Town and Durban?

Unfortunately, the extended agenda is not available in Cape Town and Durban. However, should you require more information about one of the breakaway sessions, please let your SYSYPRO representative know, and we will endeavour to share the insights from that particular session with you.

Can I go to any of the sessions on the day two agenda (Johannesburg ONLY)?

Of course you are welcome to mix and match any of the sessions to fit your agenda.
When registering (Please see the Register Now tab) you will be required to select the sessions you wish to attend. We will send you a reminder of the sessions you have registered to attend, but you are welcome to attend a different session to the one for which you initially registered.

Will I have a chance to meet with the SYSPRO Executives at the event?

Absolutely, all our senior Executives will be at the event. If you wish to set up a specific one on one meeting or discussion with a particular executive, please email us your details and supply the name of the person you would like to meet up with. We will do our utmost to accommodate your request.

If I have a technical question who can help me at the event?

We will have technical support resources at the event to answer your questions.

How do I book my hotel accommodation at the SYSPRO rate?

Please refer to the “Recommended Accommodation’ link under the Venues tab to book your accommodation at the SYSPRO rate. Please note that accommodation is for your own account.
